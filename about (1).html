The Story

Hi, I'm Fran

The Beginning

Before There Were Algorithms

Before follower counts and content strategies, I ran a makeshift online radio show — just for the love of it. No audience metrics. No monetization goals. Just me, a connection, and the pure joy of creating something and putting it out into the world.

That feeling never left me.

"It feels good to have someone to talk to."

The Journey

Streaming To Find Connection

I started streaming to overcome shyness. Gaming was the protective layer — something to do with my hands while I figured out how to just be with people online. What I discovered was that the games weren't the point. The conversations were.

The community was.

Right Now

Building In Public

Now I'm doing the whole thing differently. I'm building my brand out loud — streaming, writing, sharing the process honestly — with a community of people who get it. We're all figuring it out. Nobody here pretends otherwise.

I bring my life coaching background, my love of tech, and a very real commitment to keeping it real. No highlight reels. Just the work.

The Vision

A Place Where People Actually Talk

The Franny Pack isn't about numbers. It's a place where people genuinely come to hang out, maybe learn something, and feel less alone in the process. If you've ever felt like you don't quite belong somewhere — pull up a chair. You belong here.
